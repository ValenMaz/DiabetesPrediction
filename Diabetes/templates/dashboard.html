Hello! Lets Get Started with RiskAssess

Welcome to RiskAssess!, a risk assessment system for any interesed person to generate risk assessments for to know the state of health in relation to possible problems of diabetes. RiskAssess started off as a company that formed strong relationships with medical institutions to help them generate their assessments with ease and maximum efficiency thus saving the organization's valuable time and help to save people's life. Click below to learn about the diabetes disease that RiskAssess is capable of assessing.

Learn more »

1. Be Prepared

Have the results of your medical test results handy. You need the next medical data: Glucose and Insulin Levels, Blood Pressure, Diabetes Pedigree Function, Skin Thickness and Body Mass Index.

2. Diabetes Prediction

Navigate to the "Diabetes" section and add the corresponding details and click on predict to learn whether the patient is at the risk of this illness/disease.


3. Download Result

You can download your prediction result if you want. The downloaded file will be in pdf format.

4. Consult Your Medic

RiskAssess is a risk assessment system to generate diabetes risk assessments, but it is only a reference tool. You should talk to your doctor about your state of health and any concerns you have about that. Follow their instructions and recommendations to preserve and protect your health.
